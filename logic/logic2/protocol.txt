packet structure:
one byte per line
\

Ports:
w
wheels
5 data bytes, speeds for all 4 wheels and directions

d
dribbler
2 data bytes, speed and direction

c
charge kicker
0 data bytes, charges capacitor bank to full

k
kick
1 data byte, which capacitors to fire

d
discharge kicker
0 data bytes, discharges capacitor bank
